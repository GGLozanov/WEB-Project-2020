Pairing up our favourite peers with our favourite teachers.

TUESPairs is the latest project I have spearheaded in the mobile development field. Utilising the power of the Flutter SDK, I and three other members have created a powerful app designed to match TUES alumni with teachers for coordination regarding their final diploma projects.

The app itself includes many features:full-fledged authentication (for both teachers and students), external sign-in (social), capability to match students and teachers, chatting between matched users, push and in-app notifications, as well as full localization to Bulgarian and English!

The project utilises the Firebase cloud platform as a back-end, along with the serverless Cloud Firestore database and Cloud Storage storage.

CipherHub

This is one of my pure Android mobile apps: a cryptography translator app. It incudes a variety of ciphers with dynamic translations, custom implementations, and many quality-of-life UI features (like light and dark mode)! Ciphers like the Vigenere, Caesar, AZ126, and many others can be translated at your fingertips.

And if you don't know any of those ciphers, don't worry—I've included explanations and tutorials on how they all work.

HackTUES

I've been a very avid participant of the major hackathon HackTUES —an initiave in TUES organised by students, for students to compete and express their skills in all varieties of tech fields. I myself have participated exclusively with projects centred in the realm of game development, using Unity and C# as main tools in our development process

One of the best projects to come out of this hackathon is my team's—TeamMix's—project Reaction. It's an indie game built with the purpose of introducing young people to the realm of chemistry by quizzing them in an interactive, RPG-like setting.
