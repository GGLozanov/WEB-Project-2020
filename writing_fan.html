My Fanfiction Collage!

(hint: it's not big yet)

Welcome! Take a seat right over there, have some cocoa, and watch as you gaze in amazement at my meagre fanfiction collection! I can already understand that some of you find this really weird, and I can also agree with that. It's not like you see someone writing for such stuff every day, but I can guarantee if you just give me and the rest of the people like me a chance, that we won't disappoint.

For now, I've written almost entirely for the Gravity Falls fandom, but I also have an interest in other fandoms for shows, movies, and games I adore— including but not limted to: Avatar: The Last Airbender, BoJack Horseman, and many other smaller ones you can find in my profile description.
